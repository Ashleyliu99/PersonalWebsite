Loan Portfolio Risk Dashboard

This dashboard provides continuous visibility into changes in loan portfolio risk through delinquency trends, roll-rate migration analysis, and expected loss monitoring. It helps stakeholders detect deteriorating credit quality early, identify emerging risk patterns, and take timely risk mitigation actions.

Built with Python and Streamlit, the app enables dynamic filtering and real-time exploration, making it easy to identify risk patterns and take targeted action.
